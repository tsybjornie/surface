# KiloClaw Custom Avatar & Content Strategy

## 1. How to Replace the Default Avatar with YOURSELF
The previous Avatar used ("Anna_public_3_20240108") is a generic public model provided by HeyGen. To build true brand authority, we must clone YOU.

### The Cloning Process:
1. **Record the Training Video:** Set your phone on a tripod in a well-lit room (preferably in front of a finished Lime Paint wall).
2. **The Script:** Read any 2-minute script naturally. Look directly at the lens. Use hand gestures, but keep your body relatively still.
3. **Upload to HeyGen:** Log into your HeyGen dashboard -> "Avatars" -> "Create Custom Avatar" -> "Instant Avatar". Upload your video.
4. **Get Your ID:** Once processed, HeyGen will give you a unique `avatar_id` (e.g., `Bjorn_Custom_12345`).
5. **Update KiloClaw:** We will update `runway_heygen_live.py` to use *your* `avatar_id` instead of the public one. Now, the server will generate videos of YOU speaking 24/7.
