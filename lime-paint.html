Lime Paint

What is lime paint? It is a breathable mineral paint system that creates a soft matte texture with natural tonal movement. Unlike synthetic paints, lime paint allows moisture to evaporate from walls, making it ideal for Singapore's high-humidity environments.

Lime Paint Types

We work with curated lime paint systems, selected based on surface condition and intended use.

Pure Lime Paint

Traditional lime-based paint with high breathability and a powdery matte finish. Offers the most organic variation and movement.

Casein-Modified Lime Paint

Lime paint enhanced with natural binders for improved adhesion and durability. Provides slightly more control while retaining a mineral appearance.

Mineral Silicate-Lime Blend

A hybrid mineral system offering greater durability and washability while maintaining the visual softness of lime finishes. Suitable for higher-traffic areas.

Available Stroke Styles

Soft Cloud

Short, overlapping strokes creating gentle, cloud-like variation.

Linear Wash

Directional strokes that introduce subtle movement across the surface.

Cross-Brushed

Layered criss-cross application with visible texture and depth.

Minimal Smooth

Tighter application for the least movement possible while retaining character.

Suitability

Suitable for

  • Living rooms
  • Bedrooms
  • Feature walls
  • Low-abrasion areas

Not recommended for

  • Heavy scrubbing zones
  • Direct water exposure
  • High-traffic corridors
  • Clients expecting uniform colour
