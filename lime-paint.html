What Makes It Different

The Living Skin System

Unlike synthetic paints, our finish is not a coating applied on top of a wall — it becomes the wall.

🌬

Breathable — Vapour-Permeable

The finish does not trap moisture. It actively allows walls to release humidity, eliminating the root cause of mould, bubbling, and peeling in tropical climates.

🧪

Naturally Antimicrobial

High-alkalinity mineral lime is inherently hostile to mould, mildew, and bacteria — without synthetic biocides or chemical additives. Safe for children's rooms and allergy-sensitive households.

🍃

Carbon-Absorbing

As lime cures, it re-mineralises — absorbing CO₂ from the air and returning to stone. A wall that improves your indoor air quality as it hardens.

☕

Zero-VOC & Ethical

No synthetic plastics. No artificial resins. No animal by-products. Formulated entirely from High-Purity Mountain Minerals and Plant-Derived Cellulose — safe for nurseries, conscious homes, and green-rated buildings.

Brush Stroke Styles

Linear

All strokes in one direction — horizontal or vertical. Clean, directional movement with visible bristle marks. The most classic Grade-98 Shikkui look.

Cross-Hatch

First coat horizontal, second coat diagonal. Creates a woven, artisanal texture with depth where strokes intersect. Our recommended technique.

Stipple

Press and lift — a dabbing motion that creates a soft, cloud-like mottled effect. No directional strokes, just organic, randomised marks.

Smooth Wash

Multiple thin coats blended for minimal visible strokes. The most refined, understated finish — matte and chalky with subtle natural movement.
