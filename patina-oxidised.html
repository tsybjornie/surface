Patina & Oxidised

Capturing the beauty of time through controlled chemical reactions. Our patina finishes transform metallic and mineral surfaces into expressive, industrial statements.

Reactive Systems

Engineered finishes that utilize natural oxidation for unique visual results.

Rust & Corten Effects

Iron-based systems that develop authentic rust textures. Available in various stages of oxidation from light orange to deep charred reds.

Vertigris & Verdigrease

Copper and bronze oxidation creating iconic blue-green mineral deposits. Best for architectural accents and exterior features.

Mottled Patinas

Multi-layered mineral washes applied to metallic bases to create depth, clouding, and aged variations without full oxidation.

Suitability

Suitable for

  • Expressive feature walls
  • Industrial-themed interiors
  • Retail & Bar environments
  • Durable exterior cladding

Not recommended for

  • Clinical, sanitised environments
  • Surfaces requiring precise color-matching
  • Clients uncomfortable with material evolution
  • Budget "standard" painting projects

Patina finishes are inherently unpredictable. We guide the direction of the reaction, but the final outcome is determined by material behavior. No two walls are ever identical.
