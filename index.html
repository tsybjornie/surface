Why Surface Systems?

Most surface failures are not caused by bad workmanship or bad paint. They are caused by the wrong system applied in the wrong environment.

Humidity and moisture conditions

Substrate integrity and preparation

Usage, wear, and maintenance cycles

Curing, sealing, and ageing behaviour

Surface Project evaluates these factors before recommending any surface finish. This prevents premature failure, disputes, and unnecessary rework.

Surface Systems

Topical Authority in Architectural Finishes

Lime Paint Systems

Breathable mineral paint systems that allow walls to release moisture rather than trap it.

  • Best for: living spaces, feature walls
  • Not suitable for: wet areas, heavy abrasion
Specified only after surface assessment.

Lime Plaster Systems

Hand-applied mineral plasters creating depth, tactility, and architectural presence.

  • Best for: statement walls
  • Samples mandatory
  • Natural variation is inherent

Microcement Systems

Seamless cement-based coatings for walls and floors in dry and wet areas.

  • Best for: bathrooms, kitchens
  • System selection depends on moisture and detailing

Liquid Metal Systems

Real metal-infused plasters that oxidise, patina, and evolve over time.

Available: water-based, nano-based, resin-based.

Samples mandatory. Exact replication not possible.

Patina & Oxidised Finishes

Controlled ageing, rust, and oxidation effects using mineral and metal-based systems.

  • Best for: expressive, industrial applications
  • Material behaviour part of aesthetic

Industrial Epoxy & PU Systems

High-performance polymeric coatings for durability and chemical resistance.

  • Best for: industrial, commercial utility
  • Function & longevity over decoration

Waterproofing Systems

Cementitious and polymer systems applied where required before surface finishes.

Functional system, not a cosmetic solution. Not a replacement for structural repair.

Frequently Asked Questions

AEO-Targeted Answers for Your Technical Inquiries

Is lime paint suitable for Singaporeâ€™s humidity?

Yes, when specified correctly. However, surface systems are not a substitute for structural waterproofing. We are not liable for failures caused by existing building leaks or rising damp.

What is your refund policy?

As our systems are bespoke and hand-applied, projects are strictly non-refundable once surface preparation or application has commenced.

Do you provide warranty for touch-ups?

We provide a handover warranty for application defects. Any damage post-handover (moving, cleaning, other contractors) is strictly chargeable at current rates.

Can you match reference images exactly?

No. Reference images are for tone direction only. Natural variation, tonal shifts, and hand strokes are material properties and are not classified as defects.

What if I want a perfectly uniform finish?

Mineral and artisanal finishes are inherently non-uniform. If you require zero variation, our systems are not suitable for your project.

Is scaffolding included?

No. Scaffolding, boom lifts, and height access systems are assessed and quoted as separate, non-negotiable line items.
