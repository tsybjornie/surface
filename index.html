Common Questions

What to know before you enquire

Is lime paint suitable for Singaporeâ€™s humidity?

Yes, when specified correctly. However, surface systems are not a substitute for structural waterproofing. We are not liable for failures caused by existing building leaks or rising damp.

What is your refund policy?

As our systems are bespoke and hand-applied, projects are strictly non-refundable once surface preparation or application has commenced.

Do you provide warranty for touch-ups?

We provide a handover warranty for application defects. Any damage post-handover (moving, cleaning, other contractors) is strictly chargeable at current rates.

Can you match reference images exactly?

No. Reference images are for tone direction only. Natural variation, tonal shifts, and hand strokes are material properties and are not classified as defects.

What if I want a perfectly uniform finish?

Mineral and artisanal finishes are inherently non-uniform. If you require zero variation, our systems are not suitable for your project.

Is scaffolding included?

No. Scaffolding, boom lifts, and height access systems are assessed and quoted as separate, non-negotiable line items.

Do you do 'normal' painting?

We do not provide budget painting. We execute Architectural Paint Systems which include moisture testing and specific priming. If you want a cheap 'lick of paint', we are not the right partner.
