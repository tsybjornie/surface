Microcement

A seamless cement-based finish suitable for contemporary interiors, including walls, floors, bathrooms, and built-in elements.

Microcement Systems

Standard Microcement

A cement-based system suitable for walls and dry interior areas, offering controlled texture and a clean finish.

Reinforced Microcement

A multi-layer system with additional reinforcement for wet areas and floors. Requires substrate and moisture assessment.

Suitability

Suitable for

  • Contemporary interiors
  • Seamless floor-to-wall applications
  • Bathrooms (with proper waterproofing)
  • Commercial spaces

Not recommended for

  • Projects without moisture assessment
