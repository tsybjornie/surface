Standard Library vs. Bespoke Formulations

1. The 52-Tone Master Library: Below are our 52 standard mineral formulations. These are pre-calibrated in our Johor Lab to guarantee exact consistency across large architectural spaces. We do not alter these on-site.

2. The Bespoke Service: If you require an exact match to a fabric, furniture piece, or specific Pantone/Hex code, our lab offers full bespoke formulation. We do not "mix on the spot" with synthetic tinters—every bespoke color is formulated using pure mineral pigments over 48 hours to ensure zero color fading.

Celadonite PW · 006

The 2026 Mineral Edit is Celadonite — a quiet, architectural sage green derived from celadonite, a volcanic green earth mineral used by classical painters for centuries. Sage green is the most-searched interior colour family globally in 2026. On Level 5 Dustless Resurfacing finished walls, it does what no flat paint can — it shifts with tropical daylight, deepens at dusk, and softens every surface it touches.
